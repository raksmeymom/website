About Coffee at C minor

At C minor, coffee is not just a drink — it’s an experience. Our beans are carefully selected from the world’s best coffee-growing regions, ensuring rich flavors and bold aromas in every brew.

We roast our beans in small batches for maximum freshness, highlighting the natural characteristics of each origin. Whether you prefer a strong espresso or a smooth pour-over, we have something for every coffee lover.

Coffee is a daily ritual that connects people, sparks conversations, and fuels creativity. At C minor, we honor that tradition while embracing innovation and sustainability.
